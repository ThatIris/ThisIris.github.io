The Virtual Unattended Store

2021/ 4 months / Storyboarding + VR Design & Development

The Virtual Unattended Store is an interactive online exprience that brings a shopper through a futuristic virtual unattended store infused with Matercard retail technologies. It's intended to showcase Mastercard's frontier retail/payment solutions and weave them into a cohesive and integrated customer journey to engage potential clients, customers, and partners with immersive storytelling.

It's designed with a "Virtual First" approach, but also affords the full flexibility to "Go Hybrid", meaning it can be developed into a hybrid experience mixed with actual IOT/physical devices.

Not only is the user learning about Mastercard retail technologies in a contextual and gamified way, they can savor the "magic" of these technologies through embodied interactions when they use their actual cellphone to "enter", "add items to cart", or "check out" in the virtual store. This was enabled by allowing the phone to communicate with the desktop contents programmatically, and is a unique wow-factor of this experience.

I proposed this concept, made the initial tech demo to won leadership support, and designed + programmed the entire experience with the help of two part-time team members. This project is currently being further developed and will be integrated with the Mastercard Experience Center globally.
